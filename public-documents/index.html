About these documents

These three documents are the core governing framework of the Miccosukee Land Co-op. They apply to all properties and residents within the Co-op, including Northwest Kingdom. Understanding them is important for anyone living on or acquiring MLC land.

Additional MLC policies and documents exist and are available through the official MLC website. Members can also access a broader document set and NWK-specific analysis through the member area.

All other MLC documents — policies, procedures, and internal records — are organized in the member area and are not publicly listed here unless specifically approved for public access.
