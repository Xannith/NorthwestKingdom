Council Business Manager

Upload agendas and approved minutes, manage the council business archive index, and update the member search index.

Scaffold only. This tool will manage the Council Business archive at /member/archives/council-business/. See DOCUMENT_CONVERSION_PLAN.md for upload workflow and naming conventions.

Upload workflow

  1. Save the PDF using the naming pattern: mlc-tc-YYYYMM-[type].pdf (e.g., mlc-tc-202403-minutes.pdf)
  2. Upload to the member documents area (not to a public folder)
  3. Add an entry to the archive index with date, type, and status
  4. If converting to HTML: follow the conversion process in DOCUMENT_CONVERSION_PLAN.md
  5. Update the member search index — do not add to the public search index
