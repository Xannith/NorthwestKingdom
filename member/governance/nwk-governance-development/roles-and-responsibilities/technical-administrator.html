Role definition

Technical Administrator

Website, document storage, forms, backups, calendar tools, and publication workflows for Northwest Kingdom.

Draft role definition. This description is a starting point for discussion. See the role discussion page.

Why "Technical Administrator" instead of "Website Admin"

The scope of this role is broader than maintaining a website. The Technical Administrator is responsible for the technical infrastructure that makes NWK's documentation, communication, and operations possible — including the website, document storage, form systems, calendar tools, publication workflows, and backups. Naming it "Technical Administrator" reflects the full scope of that work.

Core responsibilities

Website and hosting

  • Maintain the NorthwestKingdom.com website and hosting configuration
  • Manage domain registration and DNS settings
  • Publish approved content updates from other role-holders and residents
  • Monitor for broken links, expired certificates, and site availability

Member access and authentication

  • Manage member accounts and access roles (member, content maintainer, records steward, admin)
  • Review and approve or decline access requests from residents
  • Revoke access when a member leaves or their role changes
  • Maintain documentation of the access model (see SECURITY_NOTES.md)

Document storage and forms

  • Maintain the document storage system (folders, naming conventions, versioning)
  • Configure and maintain forms used by residents (event requests, photo submissions, etc.)
  • Ensure documents are accessible to authorized users and not exposed publicly

Calendar tools

  • Maintain the shared calendar integration for the public and member calendar pages
  • Support residents in adding or updating events

Backups

  • Ensure regular backups of the site, documents, and member data
  • Test backup restoration periodically
  • Document backup schedule and storage location for continuity

Alternate Current publication

  • Manage the Alternate Current archive — uploading new issues, maintaining metadata, and updating the archive index
  • Support search indexing as issues are catalogued

What this role does not do

  • Does not decide what content is published (that is a governance and coordinator function)
  • Does not curate records (that is the Records Steward's role)
  • Does not represent NWK externally

Handoff documentation

The Technical Administrator maintains a private handoff document that includes: hosting provider login, domain registrar, GitHub repository access, any third-party service credentials, and steps for deploying updates. This document is stored securely and passed to the next Technical Administrator when the role changes hands.
