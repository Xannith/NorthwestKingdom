Roles and Responsibilities

The core roles that support Northwest Kingdom's ongoing organization and continuity.

Northwest Kingdom currently organizes around five roles. These roles are designed to be clearly defined, transferable, and practical — not titles for prestige but descriptions of real work that needs a clear owner.

Role Primary focus Status
Coordinator Neighborhood coordination, communication, continuity Placeholder
Treasurer Finances, shared expenses, financial records Placeholder
Bookkeeper Day-to-day financial record keeping Placeholder
Records Steward Meeting notes, decisions, document versions, institutional memory Placeholder
Technical Administrator Website, storage, forms, backups, calendar, publication workflows Placeholder

See role discussion to review or comment on these role definitions.
