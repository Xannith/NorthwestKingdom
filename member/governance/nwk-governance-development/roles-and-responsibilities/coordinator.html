Role definition

Coordinator

Overall neighborhood coordination, communication, and continuity for Northwest Kingdom.

Draft role definition. This description is a starting point for discussion, not an official policy. See the role discussion page.

What this role does

The Coordinator is the general point of contact and continuity anchor for Northwest Kingdom. This is not an authority role — it is a connective role. The Coordinator helps ensure that nothing important falls through the cracks and that residents know who to reach when something needs attention.

Core responsibilities

  • Serve as the primary point of contact for neighborhood questions and concerns
  • Coordinate communication among residents, role-holders, and the TC representative
  • Help organize and facilitate neighborhood meetings
  • Maintain a working list of open items, projects, and pending decisions
  • Support smooth transitions when other roles change hands
  • Ensure new residents are welcomed and oriented to the neighborhood

What this role does not do

  • Does not make unilateral decisions on behalf of the neighborhood
  • Does not speak for NWK in formal MLC proceedings (that is the TC Representative's role)
  • Does not manage finances (that is the Treasurer's role)
  • Does not maintain technical systems (that is the Technical Administrator's role)
