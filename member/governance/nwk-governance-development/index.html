Purpose

This section supports Northwest Kingdom's ongoing work to organize itself more clearly — not to add bureaucracy, but to preserve what works, document what matters, and make responsibilities clear enough that the neighborhood functions well across changing residents and years.

See the Lightweight Governance page for the philosophy behind this approach.

Current roles

NWK organizes around a small set of practical roles:

Coordinator

Overall neighborhood coordination, communication, and continuity.
Treasurer

Neighborhood finances, shared expenses, and financial records.
Bookkeeper

Day-to-day financial record keeping and transaction logging.
Records Steward

Meeting notes, decision records, document versions, and institutional memory.
Technical Administrator

Website, document storage, forms, backups, calendar tools, and publication workflows.
