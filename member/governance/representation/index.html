About representation

Northwest Kingdom's interests in the Miccosukee Land Co-op are represented through a member of the MLC Town Council. The Town Council is the formal governance body for the Co-op as a whole, making decisions that affect all member neighborhoods including Northwest Kingdom.

This section tracks NWK's representation — who our current TC representative is, updates from TC meetings, upcoming agenda items relevant to NWK, and a record of votes on matters affecting our neighborhood.

Residents are encouraged to submit questions or concerns for the TC representative through the Questions for TC page.
