Overview

Northwest Kingdom operates under the Miccosukee Land Co-op's governance framework. This means that MLC bylaws, covenants, restrictions, and policies apply to all NWK properties and residents. Understanding this framework is important for residents, especially for matters related to land use, shared responsibilities, and participation in Co-op decision-making.

This section documents the current MLC framework as it applies specifically to Northwest Kingdom — what rules apply, what obligations NWK residents have, what rights and limitations exist, and where the current framework has gaps that may need to be addressed.
