About NWK Gather

NWK Gather is a warm, local hub for Northwest Kingdom neighbors to coordinate potlucks, meetings, and seasonal gatherings. It is the community's shared space for planning events and staying connected.

NWK members can also access the internal member calendar for additional planning details and event coordination resources.
