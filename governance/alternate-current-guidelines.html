Alternate Current Guidelines

  1. Everything submitted must be clearly marked “For Publication.”
  2. Articles must say who submitted them.
  3. Any article naming a person in a derogatory manner will be held for the NAMED person to respond. If the response is not ready for the AC submitted, it may be held for the next AC.

Published in vol. 39 no. 4 of Alternate Current (2/20/12)
