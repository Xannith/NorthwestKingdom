Assessment Policy re Multiple but Not Identical Ownership

Assessment policy re multiple but not identical ownership

When MLC members own more than one parcel of land, but not all owners on the deeds are identical, a separate assessment applies to each parcel once a dwelling has been permitted or constructed on that parcel.  “Dwelling” as used here means any habitable structure or structure that is clearly used for lodging.

Passed 1/15/09; last sentence added pursuant to 1/16/09, 1/23/09, and subsequent  email or telephone contacts among TC members.
