Bulletin Board Guidelines

Guidelines for Bulletin Board at MLC Mailbox

Passed by Town Council April 2005

According to 4/14/05 email from Ed Deaton

First:  Please sign and date your notices.  If possible keep your postings timely – that is, no more than a month in advance of an event or activity.  Please don’t staple notices to the bulletin board.

Second: Only the poster and/or the coordinator will move or remove items from the bulletin board.

Third:  Please post notices for ongoing activities like classes or groups on paper/poster board no larger than 5×8 – it’ll make more room available for other stuff.

Finally, Co-op business and community activities take precedence over other postings and member notices take precedence over outside or commercial notices.

Addendum by Mo Malvern:  Now we have a separate bulletin board on the package cupboard for official notice of MLC meetings (TC, neighborhood groups, committees).  Please don’t use the new section for anything else.
