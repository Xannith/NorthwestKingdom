Request Member Access

For Northwest Kingdom residents and authorized household members.

Access is by invitation. Member accounts are created by the neighborhood coordinator. Self-registration is not available. Contact the coordinator directly to request an invitation.

How it works

  1. Contact the neighborhood coordinator and let them know you would like access.
  2. The coordinator will send you an invitation email from northwestkingdom.com.
  3. Click the link in the invitation email and set your password.
  4. You can then log in with your email and password.

Contact the coordinator

Contact information for current NWK coordinators is on the contact page.
